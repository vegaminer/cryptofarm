=== How to open Windows Defender settings? ===

<WRAP group>
<WRAP half column>

 1. Press [[https://www.google.com/search?q=win+logo+key&source=lnms&tbm=isch&sa=X|WIN LOGO KEY + S]] to open search dialog.
 2. Type ''threat''.
 3. Click ''Virus & threat protection'' search item.
 4. Click ''Threat history''
 5. [[windows:rigutils:windows:antivirustest.bat#threat_history|Back]] to AntiVirusTest troubleshooting.

</WRAP>
